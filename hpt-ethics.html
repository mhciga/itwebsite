HPT Ethics

International Society for Performance Improvement (ISPI)

Six Principles

Add Value

We will ensure our projects and results add value to the client, customers, and global community.

Validated Practice

We will use and promote certified practices in performance technology strategies and standards.

Collaboration

We will work in partnership with our clients and users.

Continuous Improvement

We will continually improve our proficiency in the field.


Integrity

We will treat our clients, colleagues, and the public with respect and dignity. We will be truthful in all interactions.

Uphold Confidentiality

We will maintain client confidentiality and not engage in any activities that cause a conflict of interest.
