AECT Code of Professional Ethics

Three Categories of Commitment

Individual Learner

We will respect the personal rights of individuals and treat them with respect. Our recommendations will support the learnerâ€™s pursuit of learning. We will ensure the health and safety of our learners by following procedures for evaluating and selecting materials for the learning environment.

Society

We will encourage the development of programs and media that emphasize diversity. We will accurately present facts regarding educational material and promote responsible use of communication media for learning and social purposes.

Profession

We will treat all members of the profession with respect and dignity. We are committed to providing culturally and intellectually diverse views in our publications. We will continually strive to improve professional knowledge and implement the best learning and teaching practices.
